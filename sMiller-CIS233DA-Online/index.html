Heavy Iron Workstaion

A quick page to help look at alternative ways to aquire and build very fast workstations for students and hobbiests. By utilizing workstation or server hardware from 2 or 3 "Generation" old systems, we can build machines approaching the performance of the latest Intel I5 or I7 CPUs. The latest Intel CPU's have from 6 to 18 cores. The older Xeon CPUs have 4 to 8 cores. But cost as little as $20 each.
